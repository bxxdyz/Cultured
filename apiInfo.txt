* Rest Countries (restcountries.eu):
    - https://restcountries.eu/rest/v2/alpha/{countryCode}
    - Free API with no key needed, just the above URL 
    - Potential important data points: 
        * root.capital
        * root.alpha3code
        * root.currencies.code
        * root.flag
        * root.languages.name
        * root.population
